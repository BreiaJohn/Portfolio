The Process

I pride myself in providing my clients with their desired vision for their project. My goal is to exceed all expectations and provide my clients with a quality site that represent who they are as well as their business. I believe it is very IMPORTANT for expectations to be mutual so here is how:

  • Consultation
  • Proposal
  • Project Begins
  • Project & support Delivered

Let's Chat!

Here we will schedule a time to talk where you will share your ideas of how you would like to see your site. You will share with me all of the details as well as the budget that you have to work with. At this time I will tell you what I will be able to do with the budget you share with me.

Agreements & Contracts

Here we will put everything that we both come to a mutual agreement on into a legal binding contract that will protect us both.

Let's Begin!

At this point I will begin your project and I will work on it within the time-frame that we both agree on. During this time we will choose an agreed upon day weekly where will will discuss where I am at in the process and you will be able to see what is happening. You will be able to make changes at this point but expect extended times if there are any updates or changes other that what was shared in the proposal.

Finished Product

One your project is complete I will provide you with your site and licenses affiliated with your site. If you need assistance with setting up your site on a hosting site or if your in need of a domain I will be able to assist you further for an additional fee. Additionally I will also provide available ongoing support.
